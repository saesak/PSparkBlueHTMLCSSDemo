Chicken Soup

Good for a cold day

Time needed: 5 mins

Feeds 4

Ingredients

  • 1 oz chicken broth
  • 2 noodles (must be the twisty kind)

Instructions

  1. Microwave broth for 4 minutes
  2. Add noodles
  3. Mix with a spoon

Beef Stew

A nice and hearty meal for the family

Time needed: 5 mins

Feeds 4

Ingredients

  • 1 oz beef broth
  • 2 potatoes

Instructions

  1. Microwave broth for 4 minutes
  2. Add potatoes (unpeeled)
  3. Mix with a spoon
